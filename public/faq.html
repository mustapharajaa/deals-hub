Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Deal Hub partnerships and exclusive deals

What do I get as a partner?

As a valued Deal Hub partner, you'll receive: Access to exclusive discount opportunities, subscription to our partner newsletter with industry insights, promoted posts across our social media channels, and entry into our community of entrepreneurs and business owners looking to share knowledge, funding opportunities, and mentorships.

How do I become a partner?

Send us an email at hello@dealhub.com and we'll get back to you ASAP. We'd love to feature your coupon, discount, or include your service or app in our monthly promotions. New deals are typically posted within 24 hours of approval.

What increases my chances of being featured?

We receive hundreds of applications monthly, so make sure to stand out by offering our community the best deal possible. Just like most successful communities, Deal Hub thrives when members provide genuine value to each other - the more you're willing to give, the more you'll receive in return.

My product is just an MVP. Is that okay?

Absolutely! Even an MVP or beta version can provide tremendous value to our community. As long as there are no hidden strings attached and you're offering genuine value, we're happy to consider featuring your product.

Why did we launch Deal Hub?

Deal Hub was created by entrepreneurs, for entrepreneurs. We spent too much time and money searching for the right tools and solutions for our businesses. In the startup world, every dollar and minute counts. That's why we curate only the best SaaS and tools, constantly working with our partners to ensure our community gets maximum value.

How much does it cost?

Deal Hub is completely free for both users and partners! Our platform is supported through partnerships and affiliate relationships. If you ever need additional marketing or promotional services, feel free to reach out to us at partnerships@dealhub.com

Can I suggest deals even if I'm not a partner?

Currently, only approved deals are featured on our platform. However, if you know of a company you'd love to see listed, please let us know through your favorite social platform or email us directly. We're happy to reach out to them on your behalf!

I see my competition on the site, should I still join?

Absolutely! In fact, many companies that work with us solve similar problems and often end up collaborating to create even better products and solutions. We're a community above all else, so you'll get out what you put in. May the best deal win! 🏆
