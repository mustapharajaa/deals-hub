Figma generally does not offer traditional coupon codes for discounts on its paid plans. Instead, they provide a free Starter plan for individuals and small teams to explore core features. For students and educators, Figma offers a free education plan that is equivalent to the Professional plan. If you are looking for discounts, it's worth checking if you qualify for the education plan. 
Here's a more detailed breakdown:
No Traditional Coupon Codes:
Figma typically doesn't use promotional codes or coupons like many SaaS platforms. 
Free Starter Plan:
Figma offers a free Starter plan for individuals and small teams, allowing them to test the core features without time restrictions. 
Education Plan:
Students and educators are eligible for a free education plan, which is a full version of the Professional plan, according to Figma's forum. 
Other Discounts:
While traditional coupons are not available, it's worth checking websites that specialize in student discounts or other promotional offers related to Figma. For example, Student Beans offers discounts for students.

If you are attending the Figma Config conference, you might find discount codes for tickets, says Magicul.io.
