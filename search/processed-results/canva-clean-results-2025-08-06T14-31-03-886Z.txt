Canva Savings Hacks

Does Canva offer free trials?


Yes, on top of the free basic version of Canva which you can use indefinitely, you can get a 30-day free trial of Canva Pro.

Does Canva have an email newsletter?

Yes, you can opt in to marketing emails from Canva to get new information and discounts right to your inbox.

Does Canva have a refer-a-friend program?

Yes, free users can earn Canva credits when their friends join through their referral link. Canva credits can be used to purchase premium elements for use in Canva itself.

Is there a Canva reward system for my subscription?

No, Canva does not have a reward program other than the friend referral link at this time. But if you pay for an annual subscription instead of monthly, you'll save 16% on the cost.

Does Canva offer a special student discount?

Yes, both primary and secondary students can access “Canva for Education” free through their teachers. College and university students are not currently eligible, however.

Does Canva have a discount for nonprofit organizations?

Yes, Canva offers free subscriptions for nonprofits. Check the eligibility guidelines to see if your organization qualifies.

Canva Saving Tips and Hacks

What Is Canva’s Cancellation Policy?

Canva allows Pro subscription cancellation after your current billing period. After cancellation, you can access the features of Canva Pro until your billing period is over. The company will also save your designs and brand kit, so you can access it when you subscribe to Canva Pro again. Be sure to settle all your previous payments and cancel your subscription at least a day before the expiry of your current subscription to avoid any additional charges. You will only receive a refund for wrongful charges.

What Is Canva’s Pausing Policy?

You can suspend your Canva Pro subscription for up to three months at any time if you need a break. The option is available for monthly Canva Pro plans and year plans with less than two months left to the expiry date. Your subscriptions will automatically resume after the selected pause duration, and Canva will send you email reminders before activating your subscription.

How To Contact Canva

If you have a question or a complaint about your subscription or need more information about anything, you can contact the Canva customer support team. Navigate to the get in touch page and fill out the form. Canva will reach out to you as soon as possible, depending on your issue. If you need help with specific solutions, you can also visit Canva’s help center for solutions and links to fast assistance.

About Canva

Canva was founded in 2013 by Cliff Obrecht, Melanie Perkins, and Cameron Adams. It is an Australian online design and publishing company tool used to create, share, and print presentations, social media graphics, posters, documents, business cards, invitations, logos, and other visual content. Canva aims to empower people worldwide to design anything and publish it anywhere. Anyone can use the platform to edit pictures using its myriad of features without needing any specific photo editing skills and experience. The platform utilizes a drag-and-drop interface that makes customizing templates and images uncomplicated and effortless.
