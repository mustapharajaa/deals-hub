Snapchat occasionally offers promotional ad credits to new advertisers as an incentive to try their platform. These are often tied to spending a certain amount within a set timeframe, and they don't require a specific coupon code. Instead, the offer is usually accessed through a unique link or by accepting a notification in Ads Manager. 
Here's a breakdown:
1. First-Time Advertiser Offers:
Snapchat frequently provides ad credits to new advertisers who haven't previously advertised on the platform. 
These offers typically involve spending a certain amount (e.g., $50 or $350) within a specific period (e.g., 14 days) after creating a new ad account to receive a bonus credit (e.g., $75 or $375). 
No coupon code is needed; the offer is activated when you sign up through a qualifying link and meet the spending requirements. 
These offers are usually tied to a specific ad account and currency. 
2. How to Claim Offers:
Find a qualifying link: Look for promotional links from Snapchat or its partners. 
Create an ad account: Follow the instructions to create your Snapchat Ads account. 
Accept the offer: You may need to accept the offer within Ads Manager or via email notification. 
Meet the spend requirement: Spend the designated amount within the specified timeframe. 
Enjoy your bonus credits: The credits will be applied to your account once you've met the requirements. 
3. Important Notes:
Offers are generally for first-time advertisers only. 
Ad credits usually have an expiration date. 
You can track your progress towards meeting the spend requirement in Ads Manager. 
For Snapchat+, you might receive promo codes from partners for free trials. These are redeemed on the Snapchat Support website. 
4. Redeeming Coupons (for existing advertisers):
If you have a coupon code from Snapchat (e.g., for a specific campaign), you can redeem it in Ads Manager under Billing & Payments.
Select "Redeem Coupon" and enter the code, associating it with the relevant ad account and campaign.
