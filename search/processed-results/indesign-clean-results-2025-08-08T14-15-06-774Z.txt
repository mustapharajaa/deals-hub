InDesign features

Adobe's premier tool for professional layout and print design.

Text and typography control

InDesign provides advanced typesetting tools, enabling users to precisely control text, line spacing, kerning, tracking, and more. The Character and Paragraph Styles panels allow for consistent formatting throughout a document.

Grids and guides

Essential for layout precision, these tools help designers organize content, ensure alignment, and maintain consistent spacing. Customizable grids and user-friendly guides make placing and aligning objects a breeze.

High-quality export options

InDesign supports a range of output formats, from high-resolution print PDFs to interactive digital formats. Its export capabilities ensure that designs look crisp and professional across all mediums.

Linked content

Designers can link content across InDesign documents, ensuring updates in one location automatically reflect in all linked instances. This feature is especially useful for documents that share common content, like brochures or catalogs.

Master Pages

This feature allows you to create a consistent layout for documents, setting up a design template that can be applied to multiple pages. With Master Pages, designers can ensure uniformity across a document or series of documents by setting elements like headers, footers, and page numbers.

Interactive elements

Beyond static print layouts, InDesign supports the creation of interactive digital documents, including buttons, hyperlinks, animations, video integration, and slide transitions. This feature is ideal for designing interactive PDFs, e-books, and digital magazines.

Adaptive design tools

With Liquid Layout and Alternate Layout, designers can easily adapt content for various page sizes and orientations. These tools are crucial for creating responsive designs that work across multiple devices and print formats.

Integrated with Adobe suite

InDesign is seamlessly integrated with other Adobe software, such as Photoshop and Illustrator. This allows for effortless importing of graphics and images, maintaining their original quality and making edits directly within InDesign.
